WHY THIS QUIZ IS DIFFERENT

Real data. Honest tradeoffs.

Most retirement quizzes pick the answer first.

You answer a few mood-board questions and they hand you a sunny coastal town. The math behind the recommendation is unclear, the cities pool is small, and the rankings rarely update.

This quiz starts from a 100-city database, each city scored on the same 10 dimensions using sources like Zillow ZHVI for housing, CMS for Medicare costs, KFF for Medigap, BLS Consumer Expenditure Survey for non-housing budget, and federal climate-risk data. The methodology is published. The figures are dated.

Your quiz answers re-weight the rankings to reflect what you care about, then surface tradeoffs honestly. If a top match has hurricane risk or no major airport, the profile says so.

FAQ

Common questions

How long does the quiz take?
About 2 minutes. The quiz is 7 short steps covering what matters most: budget, healthcare, climate, walkability, and a few must-haves.
Is it free? Do I need to sign up?
Yes, it is free, and no signup is required to see your results. You can optionally provide your email to receive a more detailed report, but it is not necessary to take the quiz or view rankings.
How many cities are in the rankings?
100 US cities, scored across the same 10 dimensions. The list spans every region and a wide range of budget tiers, climates, and city sizes.
What makes this better than a "Where should I retire?" personality quiz?
Personality quizzes match you to a vibe. This quiz ranks 100 real cities by real data, with every score and figure backed by published methodology. You see exact budget estimates, healthcare ratings, climate risk scores, and tax-friendliness rankings for each city.
Can I compare my top cities head-to-head?
Yes. After the quiz, open any city profile for the full 10-dimension scorecard, then jump to a side-by-side comparison with any other city in the database.
Does it include international retirement destinations?
Not currently. The database focuses on US cities where retirement data is reliable and directly comparable. International destinations may be added later.

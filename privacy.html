Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Who We Are

RetireMeHere.com is an independent retirement city matching tool that helps you find the best places to retire based on your personal priorities. The site is operated independently and is not affiliated with any real estate company, financial institution, or sponsored advertiser.

What Information We Collect

We collect your email address when you request a free report. We do not collect your name, phone number, payment information, or any other personal data.

When you take the quiz, your answers are processed entirely in your browser and are not stored or transmitted to our servers.

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This collects anonymous data such as pages visited, time on site, and general location (city/region level). No personally identifiable information is collected through Analytics.

How We Use Your Information

Your email address is used solely to deliver the free report you requested. We may occasionally send relevant updates about RetireMeHere.com. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send.

Who We Share Your Data With

We use MailerLite to manage email delivery. Your email address is stored in MailerLite's system. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any other third parties.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your email address at any time by emailing us at retiremehere@gmail.com or by clicking unsubscribe in any email we send you.

Cookies

We use cookies through Google Analytics for anonymous site usage tracking only. No advertising cookies are used on this site.

No Sponsored Results

RetireMeHere.com does not accept payment to feature or rank any city. All scores are based on independent research. Our results are never influenced by advertisers.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email us at retiremehere@gmail.com.

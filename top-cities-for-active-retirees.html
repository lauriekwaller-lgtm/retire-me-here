For Active Retirees

The best US cities to retire if fitness is your priority.

From our curated database of U.S. retirement cities — a sampling, not an exhaustive ranking. We add new cities regularly.

Cities with serious strength training, Pilates, yoga, and aging-athlete programming — where staying strong well into your 70s and 80s is the local norm, not the exception.

What we looked for: real strength training infrastructure (not just chain gyms), depth across modalities (Pilates, barre, yoga, recovery), and the presence of explicit aging-athlete programming — like dedicated 50+ classes, "Strength for Longevity" gyms, and Menopause-Pilates certifications. Cities with strong outdoor recreation but thin indoor fitness scenes are covered on our Top Cities for Hikers page instead.

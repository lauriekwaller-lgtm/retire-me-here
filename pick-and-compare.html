Methodology & sources

Median home values are Zillow Home Value Index (ZHVI) typical-value estimates. Eight cities (Indianapolis, Memphis, Philadelphia, Pittsburgh, San Antonio, St. Louis, St. Paul, and Wilmington DE) use retiree-target neighborhood data rather than citywide figures and are shown as ranges.

Monthly cost bands are directional composite estimates drawn from multiple cost-of-living sources. Use them as a gauge for relative differences between cities, not as a personal budget. Verify with your own research before relocating.

Budget tier is our 1-of-5 classification: 1 means lowest cost of living in our 100-city database, 5 means highest.

Dimension scores (1 to 10) are RetireMeHere's proprietary scoring across our 100-city database, drawing on sources like US News hospital rankings, FlightConnections airport route data, BestPlaces and ERI cost data, state revenue departments for tax friendliness, county property tax records, FBI/ERI crime data, and editorial research on walkability, outdoor recreation, and community character.

## The page, briefly
The honest hook: this is the closest pairing we have scored. Same budget
tier, same monthly estimate, identical on 7 of 10 dimensions. Only THREE ✓:
Bloomington's home ($315K vs $333K) and insurance ($2,887 vs $4,042, the
lowest insurance figure on the entire site), and Lexington's healthcare
(9 vs 7, UK HealthCare). Everything else is character, carried in prose.

catch here is WINTER (warm-winter 3 and 4), and the upside is the
insurance/resilience math coastal cities can't touch. This reframes the
whole Midwest cluster as "real seasons, real affordability, no hurricanes."
